Judges Spotlight

Ron Gula

President and Co-Founder Gula Tech Adventures
Co-Founder Tenable Networks

Gula Tech Adventures

Since 2017, Ron has been a key figure in cybersecurity, investing in start-ups and supporting nonprofits. He mentors founders on GTA's portfolio boards and contributes to key initiatives. From 2002-2016, Ron co-founded and led Tenable Network Security as CEO, growing it to 20,000 customers, $100M in revenue, and securing $300M in funding, culminating in a $3B IPO in 2018.

A cyber pioneer, Ron developed Dragon, an early commercial IDS, and served as a penetration tester at the NSA, also deploying honeypots for the U.S. Defense Department in the 1990s.

Troy Wilkinson

Global F500 CISO, Former CISO

Global F500 CISO, Former CISO

Troy Wilkinson started his career as a law enforcement officer, leading a Joint Terrorism Task Force and specializing in cyber and child exploitation crimes. He trained police in Kosovo on cyber investigations and worked with the UN and EU on political corruption and terrorism cases.

As former Global CISO at Interpublic Group and CISO at International Cruise & Excursions, he co-founded Tego Cyber Inc. and serves on its Board. A global speaker, co-author of an Amazon Best Seller, and advisor to VCs and cybersecurity startups, he also contributes to national publications and conferences.

Malcolm Harkins

Chief Security & Trust Officer, Hidden Layer
Former CISO Intel

Hidden Layer

Malcolm Harkins is Chief Security and Trust Officer at HiddenLayer with over 20 years in info security. He's held leadership roles at Intel, Cylance, and others, and authored books on risk management and security. Harkins has received awards from RSA, ISC2, and more.

He's testified before the FTC and U.S. Senate and is a Fellow at the Institute for Critical Infrastructure Technology, advising Congress and federal agencies. Harkins holds a B.A. in economics from UC Irvine and an MBA from UC Davis and has taught at UCLA and Susquehanna University.

Rinki Sethi

VP & CISO

Rinki Sethi is a cybersecurity leader with executive experience at companies like BILL, Twitter, Rubrik, IBM, Palo Alto Networks, Intuit, and eBay. She currently serves on the board of Vaultree, and previously served on the boards of Women in CyberSecurity, One Prosper and ForgeRock.

Rinki advises cybersecurity startups and is an active angel investor. She is also a frequent contributor to major publications like Fortune, Forbes, and WSJ and a keynote speaker at global conferences.

Damian Chung

Business Information Security Officer

Netscope

Damian Chung is a cybersecurity leader with 25+ years of experience. As VP of Cyber Defense at Netskope, he oversees global SOC, Threat/Vulnerability Management, and Operational Threat Intelligence. He leads the Netskope Customer Zero program and is focused on AI integrations to enhance security response and reduce risk.

Previously, Damian led Cybersecurity Engineering/Architecture at Dignity Health, developing a healthcare security roadmap. He also served as VP of IT, Cloud Security & Compliance for a healthcare tech company, building HIPAA-compliant cloud services. Damian holds an MSc from Arizona State University.
